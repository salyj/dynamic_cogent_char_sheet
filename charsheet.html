ATTRIBUTES

STR:

(Strength)

For every point in STRENGTH

severity in injury can be reduced once in every conflict resolution and the player recieves an additional die for all attack and defense rolls

REF:

(Reflex)

For every point in REFLEX

Turn Priority is Higher at the beginning of combat encounters and the player recieves an additional die for all attack and defense rolls

INT:

(Intelligence)

For every point in INTELLIGENCE

recieve 3 additional skill points to spend in character creation and the player recieves an additional die for all attack and defense rolls
